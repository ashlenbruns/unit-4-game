Tree Hugger Game!

You will be given a random Target Number at the start of the game.

Hug a tree below by clikcing on it. You will gain a specific amount of karma points for each tree you hug.
The value of each tree hug is hidden until you click on it, and it changes with each new game.

You win the game by matching your total karma points to the Target Number, but don't go over or you will lose.
